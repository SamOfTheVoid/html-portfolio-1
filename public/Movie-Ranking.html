Sam's Favorite Movies

The top three films ever, as recognized by yours truly

The Princess Bride

Honestly, what's not to love? This film is a classic by nearly anyone's standards.

The Ladies' Man

Maybe a little crude by today's standards, but Tim Meadows is absolutely hilarious.

Multiplicity

An underrated comedy classic from the 90's. Michael Keaton plays four of the funniest parts of his career.
